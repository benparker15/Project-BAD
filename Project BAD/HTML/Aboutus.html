History

Fuze Creative was established in Jakarta in January 2021 by Indonesia sound designer Alexander and Indonesian electronic musician, and the website was launched in June 2021.

It was originally intended to allow allows every musicians, composers, and music producer to share their ideas and innovation to collaborate by facilitating the sharing and discussion of recordings, but later transformed into a publishing tool for music distribution through Fuze Creative community. Fuze Creative began to challenge the dominance of Myspace as a platform for musicians to distribute their music
